Shifting Proximitie

31 OCT 2021

Current exhibition

Shifting Proximities explores human experience and interaction in the face of social and technological change.

The eight immersive, multi-sensory installations in our inaugural exhibition have been created in collaboration with artists, designers, technologists, scientists and musicians. The artworks fuse creative ideas with groundbreaking academic research and technological innovation; four of which have been commissioned by and will premiere at Nxt Museum.
